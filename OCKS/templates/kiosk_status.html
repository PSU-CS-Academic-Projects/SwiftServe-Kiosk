Cancellation and Refund Rules

Eligibility: You can cancel only while the order is in Pending status and within the allowed cancel window.

After Preparation Starts: Once the order moves to Preparing/Ready/Completed, cancellation is disabled.

Refunds: If payment is already marked Paid and cancellation is accepted, payment status is changed to Refund Requested for staff processing.
